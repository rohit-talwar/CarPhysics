---------------------------------------
		CarPhysics
---------------------------------------
		         - Rohit Talwar

A project made to explore the different
aspects of the physics applied in a 
real world car-racing game. 

Simple camera views can be seen- demon-
strating the power of the lookat func

To play

w - acceleration
s - brakes
a - left
d - right

1 to 8 to take a view from the different
angles.

9 for Helicopter view - drag the mouse 
to change the camera angle. 
Scroll up/down to zoom in or out.
